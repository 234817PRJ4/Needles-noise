Rock your tattoos!

What We Do?

Needles & Noise is the go-to platform for tattoo enthusiasts and artists to connect and create pieces inspired by their shared passion for hard rock and art. Here, you can find inspiration for your own unique designs, browse artist catalogs, and discover the one whose style speaks to you. It's easy — just find your artist, connect, book, and rock on.

Our Values 

Our platform is built to connect through the same values that fuel rock fans—raw passion, real connection, and a love for bold expression . Every word we use is chosen to bring that vision to life and speak directly to the heart of the rock community. Giving a stage to rock-inspired tattoo artists isn’t just something we do—it’s something we believe in. That’s why the values on our website aren’t just words—they’re the backbone of everything we stand for.                     

Authenticity 
We appreciate the freedom to be yourself and reject mainstream norms
Individuality 
Hard rock celebrates individuality. Tattoos are the prime example of personal expression.
Sense of community
We believe in forming deep connections through shared interests, attending concerts, and participating in discussions. We want to give back to the community and the artists in it. 
Uplifting small artists
We focus on supporting small artists. We reject commercialized art and don't believe in big brands.
